Vicious Apparel

Personal Fashion Brand

Project Type

Brand Identity, Graphic Design

Role

Designer

Overview

As a class assignment we were asked to create a fashion brand and the brand identity associated with it. I chose to create a streetwear brand that drew inspiration from 1970s glam rock New York City

Discovery

I had an idea of what I needed my brand to look like but it was necessary to pinpoint the motifs that would be present throught the identity. I centered the idea around glam rock, specifically the flam rock of New York City. Lou Reed, David Bowie and Elton John were some of the artist I looked towards for inspiration. New York, Campy and Lou Reed' "Vicious" were what I centred the brand around. In fact, Vicious became the name.

Concept

I have a personal bias towards orange (as you may have already seen), so I chose that as my main brand colour. I wanted the feeling and texture of the brand to stay somewhat gritty but not so much that it carries a dated look.

Results

Overall, the outcome was what I had in mind in the beginning. It was streetwear but didn't take itself too seriously. I think that's what the flower logomark evokes. In total 8 pieces of marketing materials were created for the brand. These include a business card, shopping bag, tags and a lookbook for the season.

Logo

The primary goal of the "Vicious" logog is to eveoke a vintage feel akin to the glam rock era that the name is inspired by. The texture is permanent so as to not lose this feeling across any medium. The word mark itself may be left empty as well so that others may be inspired to fill in the blank.
